SPORTS

Diving
Diving is a form of movement downwards, either from air to ground or to water. Diving is also a sport. Diving is often done from a springboard or an elevated platform.
Golf
Golf is a game played in an open field where the golfer plays his golf ball into a hole by using different types of clubs
Boxing
Boxing is one of the world's oldest combat sports. It is held in a ring and two boxers
Sailing
The sport of sailing is racing other boats around a track or course which is marked with floating buoys or other fixed marks. Boats can be from small dinghies to large yachts
Baseball
Baseball is a bat-and-ball sport played on a field by two teams against each other. In baseball, a player on one team throws a small round ball at a player on the other team, who tries to hit it with a bat.
